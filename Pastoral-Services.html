Pastoral Services

At Filipino Christian Church Abu Dhabi, Our Pastors Are Dedicated To Shepherding, Teaching,
And Equipping God’s People For His Glory.

Child Dedication

Child Dedication at Filipino Christian Church is a joyful celebration where parents publicly commit to raising their child according to God’s Word and guidance. It is not just a baptism, it is an spiritual milestone — a declaration of faith and dedication by the family and the church community. Our pastoral team leads this special service with prayer and encouragement, blessing the child and supporting the parents in their role as the child’s first spiritual mentors. Together, we entrust the child’s future into the loving hands of God.

Wedding Vows

Whether you need someone to pray with you, officiate a wedding, conduct a dedication, or offer encouragement during difficult times, our pastors are committed to walking with you in love and humility. We believe in building strong, Christ-centered relationships that reflect the heart of our Shepherd — Jesus Christ.
