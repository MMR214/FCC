PASTOR BEN HUR PINEDA

Our Pastors

“Join us and let us all experience the love and grace of God in Jesus Christ.”

PASTOR BEN HUR PINEDA

Associate Pastor

Pastor Ben Hur has been faithfully serving in pastoral ministry for 36 years. For the past 15 years, he has served as the Associate Administrative Pastor of the Filipino Christian Church in Abu Dhabi, overseeing the International Worship Service. He completed his undergraduate studies with a Bachelor of Theology (B.Th.) and earned his postgraduate degree with a Master of Divinity (M.Div.). He is married to Rhoda Gasalao, and they are blessed with two children, Rainbow and Trinity.

Pastor Ben Hur is passionate about leading people to a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and helping them grow in their faith by equipping them to accurately understand and live out the truths of God's Word.

Pastoral Services

Whether you need someone to pray with you, officiate a wedding, conduct a dedication, or offer encouragement during difficult times, our pastors are committed to walking with you in love and humility. We believe in building strong, Christ-centered relationships that reflect the heart of our Shepherd — Jesus Christ.
