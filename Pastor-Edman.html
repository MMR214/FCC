PASTOR EDMAN CHESTER OCAMPO

Associate Pastor Cum Youth Pastor

Pastor Edman Chester S. Ocampo is a passionate servant of God, currently serving as the Youth and Associate Pastor at the Filipino Christian Church (FCC) in Abu Dhabi. He is dedicated to shepherding and discipling the next generation of believers. He holds a degree in Theology, majoring in Pastoral Leadership, from Bethel Bible College. His ministry is marked by a deep heart for worship, discipleship, and community building. Before his current role, he served as a Music Production Coordinator at Christ Commission Fellowship (CCF) and as a Music Teacher at Green Hills Christian Fellowship (GCF). Pastor Edman is joyfully married to Chamy Dy Ocampo, and they are blessed with a daughter, Selah Eunice.

With a background in both pastoral leadership and worship ministry, Pastor Edman brings a unique blend of administrative skill, pastoral care, and musical passion to his calling. His ministry is focused on pursuing God’s vision for the church and equipping believers to live Christ-centered lives.

Pastoral Services

Whether you need someone to pray with you, officiate a wedding, conduct a dedication, or offer encouragement during difficult times, our pastors are committed to walking with you in love and humility. We believe in building strong, Christ-centered relationships that reflect the heart of our Shepherd — Jesus Christ.
