Know More About Us

We are a vibrant community of believers united by our faith in Jesus Christ and our commitment to live according to His teachings. As a Filipino Christian Church, we embrace our cultural heritage while focusing on our mission to glorify God, share the Gospel, and support one another in love and unity. We believe that the church is not just a building—it is a family. Whether you are a lifelong Christian or just beginning your spiritual journey, we welcome you with open hearts. Our church exists to nurture faith, encourage service, and be a light in our local and global communities.

Statement of Faith

We believe that the Bible, God's inspired Word, is the final authority for faith and life. There is one God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—eternally existing and perfect in all ways. Jesus Christ, fully God, and fully man, was born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose again, and now intercedes for us. The Holy Spirit convicts regenerates, and empowers believers for godly living. All people are sinners in need of salvation, which comes only through faith in Jesus Christ. We believe in His imminent return, the resurrection of all people—eternal life for believers and judgment for unbelievers. The Church, His body, is called to share the Gospel, disciple believers, and serve in love, bringing glory to God.

Mission

“ We endeavor to lead people to a saving relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ and into membership in His family, help them grow into Christ-like maturity, equip them for their ministry in the church and life mission in the world, in order to magnify God. “

Church History

The Filipino Christian Church (FCC) began in 1984 as the Filipino Christian Fellowship (FCF) through the vision of Pastor Moses and Sister Nancy Jesudass. It started with a small group of Filipino Christians gathering for worship and fellowship. Over the years, FCF grew in numbers and spiritual depth, leading to its establishment as a formal church in Abu Dhabi.

In 1997, FCF was officially registered with the UAE government, marking a significant milestone in its journey. The church has since expanded its ministries, reaching out to the Filipino community and beyond, providing spiritual guidance, support, and a sense of belonging.

Today, FCC continues to thrive as a vibrant community of faith, committed to serving God and the people of Abu Dhabi. Its rich history is a testament to God's faithfulness and the dedication of its members.
