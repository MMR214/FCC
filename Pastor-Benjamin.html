PASTOR BENJAMIN E. MASIGLAT, JR.

Our Pastors

“Join us for worship, grow in faith, and discover the joy of serving together. Whether you’re exploring faith, seeking community, or looking for a church to call home, you belong here. Come as you are—we can’t wait to meet you!”

REV. BENJAMIN E. MASIGLAT, JR.

Senior Administrative Pastor

Pastor Benjie is an ordained pastor of the Unida Evangelical Church and a graduate of the Asian Theological Seminary, Batch 1984, with a Master of Divinity degree majoring in Theological Studies. With 47 years of faithful service as a church worker and pastor, he currently serves as the Senior Administrative Pastor of the Filipino Christian Church in Abu Dhabi, UAE—a position he has held since 2004. He is also the current chairman of the board of trustees of the Evangelical Church in Abu Dhabi. Pastor Benjie has been happily married to Fe Atienza Masiglat of Barangay, Lumil, Silang, Cavite, for 40 years, and they are blessed with four children and eight grandchildren.

Pastor Benjie's Personal Mission Statement: I am a pastor called by God, committed to leading others to Christ, nurturing them into faithful disciples, and equipping them to become cutting-edge leaders of their time, leaving behind a lasting legacy of faithfulness to God for the next generation.

Pastoral Services

Whether you need someone to pray with you, officiate a wedding, conduct a dedication, or offer encouragement during difficult times, our pastors are committed to walking with you in love and humility. We believe in building strong, Christ-centered relationships that reflect the heart of our Shepherd — Jesus Christ.
